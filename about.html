Chris Langford MA, LPC Co-Owner

Chris has been working with children and families in the mental health field since 2007 and has been working as a trauma informed clinician since 2015. In his time treating individuals who are struggling with difficult memories and experiences he has helped adults, adolescents and children from all walks of life and representing every ethnic and religious background and every gender and orientation. Chris is an EMDR trained clinician.

Jaime Langford MSW, LCSW Co-Owner

Jaime has been working in the field since 2007 and has experiences treating children and families struggling with a multitude of difficulties. She has experience helping adolescents and young adults struggling with depression, anxiety, obsessive compulsive disorder, life transitions, and difficult relationships. Jaime also has experience helping children, adolescents, and families to cope with symptoms of Autism Spectrum Disorder. Jaime is trained in Cognitive Behavior Therapy.
